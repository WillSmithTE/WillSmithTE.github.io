Love knowing the temperature outside? Hate getting that information instantly? Try Not-That-Temp now!

In the first search bar enter your postcode, suburb, town, city or country. Optionally, also specify your country in the second search bar. To find out what the temperature isn't in that location, hit 'Search'; if that's not enough for you, keep hitting 'New Temp' to get more and more temperatures which do not describe the current climate in the chosen location. Who thought getting the weather could be so fun?!
